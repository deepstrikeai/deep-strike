DeepStrikeAI — Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

Overview

DeepStrikeAI ("the App") is developed by Kanupriya Mehta. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Data We Collect

  • Player ID — A randomly generated anonymous identifier stored locally on your device to save your game progress.
  • Game Data — Game statistics, scores, and progress stored locally and in Firebase for multiplayer features.
  • Analytics — Anonymous gameplay events stored in Firebase. No personal data is included.
  • Crash Reports — Anonymous crash data to fix bugs (via Sentry).

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

Advertising

This app displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. You can opt out via your device's ad settings.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not store payment information.

Children's Privacy

This app is rated 9+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: sendyourfeedbackhere@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
