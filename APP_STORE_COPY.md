## Description (Full)

Command your fleet. Sink theirs. Welcome to Deep Strike — the next evolution of naval warfare.

**TACTICAL BATTLESHIP COMBAT**
Classic grid-based gameplay meets modern strategy. Place your fleet, hunt enemy ships, and command devastating special abilities. Six unique commanders. One battlefield. Pure tactical domination.

**VOICE COMMAND YOUR FLEET**
Call out coordinates by voice. "Fire B7." "Use ability." "Switch grids." The only battleship game where you command like a real admiral. Hands-free warfare has arrived.

**6 COMMANDERS. 6 ABILITIES.**
• Captain Gruffbeard — Broadside barrage across multiple columns
• Admiral Nova — Sonar sweep reveals hidden ships
• Captain Bubbles — Chaos torpedo strikes random targets
• Commander Zero — Radar ping scans entire rows
• Admiral Blackwood — Full broadside devastates the grid
• Captain Coral — Depth charge carpet-bombs in a cross pattern

Each commander has unique personality, taunts, and AI-powered narration. Level them up, unlock new commanders, earn rewards.

**MULTIPLAYER WARFARE**
Real-time 2-player battles with ranked ELO matchmaking. Challenge friends or face opponents worldwide. Turn-based strategy meets live competition.

**STORY CAMPAIGNS**
Operation North Star. Operation Steel Tide. Hunt rogue commanders across 10 tactical missions. Earn stars based on accuracy. Perfect your strategy.

**SEASONAL CONTENT**
Battle Pass with 20 tiers of rewards. Weekly challenges. Daily puzzles. Commander shards to unlock. Fresh content every season.

**GAME MODES**
• Classic — Traditional turn-based battleship
• Salvo — Fire multiple shots per turn
• Daily Challenge — New puzzle every 24 hours
• Campaign — Story-driven missions
• Ranked Multiplayer — Climb the leaderboard

**PREMIUM FEATURES**
Free: Captain Gruffbeard, Classic mode, single player
Deep Strike Command ($2.99): All 6 commanders, Salvo mode, multiplayer, campaigns, ranked play

No ads. No subscriptions. One purchase unlocks everything.

Built for iPhone and iPad. Optimized for dark mode. Supports landscape and portrait.
