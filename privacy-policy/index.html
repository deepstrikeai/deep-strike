Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Deep Strike ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Deep Strike mobile application.

TL;DR: Deep Strike does not sell your data. Most data is stored locally on your device. We only collect minimal data needed for multiplayer features and use industry-standard encryption for all network communication.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require account creation or personal information to use Deep Strike. The app generates an anonymous Player ID when you first launch it.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Player ID: A randomly generated UUID stored locally on your device to track your progress
  • Game Data: Commander progress, Battle Pass status, campaign completion, and settings are stored locally using AsyncStorage
  • Multiplayer Data: When you play online, game state (ship placements, moves) is stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Leaderboard Data: Your anonymous Player ID and ELO rating are stored in Firebase for ranked multiplayer
  • Push Tokens: Device push notification tokens (if you enable notifications) for turn-based multiplayer alerts

1.3 Voice Data

If you use voice commands:

  • Voice input is processed locally on your device using system speech recognition APIs
  • We do not record, store, or transmit your voice data to our servers
  • Voice recognition requires microphone permission, which you can revoke in Settings

1.4 Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Purchase receipts are validated through Apple/Google APIs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress, commander unlocks, and settings
  • Enable real-time multiplayer gameplay and matchmaking
  • Display leaderboards and calculate ELO ratings
  • Send push notifications for your turn in multiplayer games (if enabled)
  • Restore your purchases across devices
  • Improve gameplay balance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

The following data is stored only on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Premium unlock status
  • Commander ownership, XP, levels, and shards
  • Battle Pass progress and claimed rewards
  • Campaign mission completion and stars
  • Weekly challenge progress
  • Settings (grid theme, voice-only mode)

3.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase)

Multiplayer game data is stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules that:

  • Prevent unauthorized access to game state
  • Limit data modification to valid game actions
  • Use encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Automatically delete abandoned games after 7 days

3.3 Data Retention

  • Active games: Stored until the game completes
  • Completed games: Kept for 30 days for replay functionality
  • Leaderboard data: Retained for the current season (90 days)
  • Local data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We only share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Multiplayer opponents: Your anonymous Player ID and game moves are visible to your opponent
  • Leaderboards: Your anonymous Player ID and ELO rating are publicly visible
  • Service providers: Firebase (Google Cloud) hosts our multiplayer infrastructure
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Children's Privacy

Deep Strike is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Third-Party Services

Deep Strike uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud database for multiplayer features - Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: In-app purchase processing - Apple | Google
  • System Speech Recognition: Local voice command processing (no data transmitted)

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your Player ID in Settings
  • Delete: Use "Delete Account Data" in Settings to erase all local and cloud data
  • Opt-out: Disable voice commands, push notifications, or multiplayer features at any time
  • Export: Your data is minimal and non-personal; contact us if you need a copy

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

9. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access and portability
  • Right to rectification and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing

Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in the United States and other countries where Firebase operates. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Displaying an in-app notice
  • Posting the new policy at this URL
